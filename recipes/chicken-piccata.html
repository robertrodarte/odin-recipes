Chicken Piccata

Description:

Chicken Piccata is a pasta dish that taste tart due to the lemon juice, pickled capers and includes browned chicken breast cutlets that are served with a sauce of butter.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5lbs, skinless chicken breast halves or 4 to 8 chicken cutlets
  • 0.5 cup all-purpose flour
  • 0.25 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • 0.25 cup extra virgin oil
  • 4 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 0.5 cup low-sodium chicken stock, or dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 0.25 cup capers, drained
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsely
  • Optional: Throw in what you think might make it special!

Steps:

  1. Prepare the chicken cutlets
  2. Dredge the cutlets
  3. Brown the cutlets
  4. Add the stock (or wine), lemon juice, and capers
  5. Whisk in the remaining butter
  6. Serve immediately
  7. Enjoy!
